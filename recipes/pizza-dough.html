Pizza Dough

Roberta's delicious and easy to make pizza dough

This recipe, adapted from Roberta's, the pizza and hipster haute-cuisine utopia in Bushwick, Brooklyn, provides a delicate, extraordinarily flavorful dough that will last in the refrigerator for up to a week. It rewards close attention to weight rather than volume in the matter of the ingredients, and asks for a mixture of finely ground Italian pizza flour

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plus 1 tablespoon 00 flour
  • 1 cup plus 1 tablespoon and 2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea slat
  • 3/4 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
